Orc Siege Tower Defense Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Early Day ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (each, a "User") of the mobile game Orc Siege Tower Defense.

1. Personal Data and Direct Collection

We do not directly collect, store, or share any personal data or personally identifiable information from our Users. The Application does not require any account creation or registration to play.

2. Third-Party Services and Data Collection

While we do not collect personal data directly, the Application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These third-party services include:

  • Google Play Services: Used for app functionality, updates, and core Android integration.
  • Google Play Billing Service: Used to process secure in-app purchases. We do not see or store your financial details, credit card numbers, or billing addresses; all transactions are securely handled by Google.
  • Advertising Providers, such as Google AdMob: Used to display in-game advertisements. These ad providers may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and generalized location data to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

We encourage you to read the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more information on how they handle your data.

3. Children's Privacy

Our Application complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and GDPR regulations. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided information through third-party networks without their consent, they should contact us immediately.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We have the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage Users to frequently check this page for any changes.
