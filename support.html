Common Help

Ads or rewards did not load

Check your internet connection, restart the game, and try again after a short wait. Reward availability may vary by region and ad inventory.

Purchase did not appear

Make sure you are signed into the same app store account used for the purchase. Restart the game and check whether the item or ad-free mode is active.

Progress was lost

Game progress is stored locally on your device. Removing the app or clearing app data may remove local progress.
